About Bitcoin & Current Market

Bitcoin is an independent digital currency in which transactions are recorded in the blockchain that is public and contains records of each and every transaction that takes place. Bitcoin was invented by an anonymous founder known as Satoshi Nakamotoin 2009, is traded by individuals with cryptographic keys that act as wallets.

Initially Bitcoin was referred as a peer-to-peer electronic cash but the technology has evolved to emphasize being a settlement layer rather than a payment network, has remained the largest cryptocurrency by market cap.
