Binding Escrow

A buyer or seller can create escrow transactions, if a buyer sends funds it indicates their agreement to the terms of the escrow agreement. The seller indicates agreement to the terms by either a) creating the escrow transaction, or b) shipping or delivering a product or service to the buyer. In the event of a dispute both parties can present testimony and evidence to a a panel of 3 judges who will decide the case outcome which will be one of the following:
1. Refunding the funds to the buyer,
2. Releasing the funds to the seller,
3. A 50/50 split of the funds between the buyer and seller, or
4. In the rare case a decision can't be reached, the funds will be released to Section 8 Company [in Indian] registered charity of our choice and the transaction ID published on this website.
5. The results of the dispute are final and cannot be appealed.
